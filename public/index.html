About The App

About The App

Disc Golf Growth - DGG - is a training buddy, an analytics coach, a close friend, and a helpful peer.

DGG Trains With You

Recording Each Session:
  • Putting Practice or Strict Routine? Metrics or Graphics? Train or Study? We Got It All!
  • Limited time to train and play? Want to know which exercises have a bigger impact on performance? We Do That!

DGG Reveals The Story Behind Your Game and Performance

  • Graphics created from YOUR training and playing data
  • Identify correlations between performance, training, and well-being

DGG Cares

  • Check in on your emotional and physical well-being twice daily
  • Metrics to breakdown how your game play influence your mental-state and vice-versa
  • Providing tips and lessons from other users sharing more than just suggestions
  • Tips break down how they learned something and what it did to help their performance - All supported by hard data

Disc Golf Growth Features

DGG is loaded with features for the curation of your training and game performance metrics.

Disc Collection

Add Disks to your collection to be selected from during the exercise, throw, and game forms

Instructional Media

Keep track of which articles, blogs, and YouTube videos you use in the growth of your disc golf game

Training Routine Tracker

Input each of your training exercises including context specific forms for practices such as free putting, 5-Up, 11’s or the Towel Exercise.

Well-being Survey

A twice daily mental and physical well-being survey can be taken directly in the application. We will use this data to identify effects on your Disc Golf Growth and performance.

Collect Throws

How often do you just go to an open field and throw? Not often enough. Next time bring Disc Golf Growth with you or enter your results when you get home, we’ll be there.

Data Visualizations

What is all this data for? Insights! The Data Visualization section shows you the hard evidence behind HOW MUCH your game has improved, WHAT training and instructional media has had the biggest influence on you, and HOW you can keep growing your disc golf game.

Tips

The Tips section of the application provides a unique perspective on how to grow improve your game. Here you can learn just how someone learned something, see a breakdown of the resources that taught them or improved their game, then follow their steps in search of similar results.

DGG Uses

On The Field On The Course
Add Disk

Manage Disk Collection

Select Disk when entering data to know how you performed with each disk. Quickly add new disks to your collection using the Add Disk utility

Add Throw

Collect Field Work Data

Going to an open field to throw is an important exercise in growing depth to your drive

Instructional Media

Track Growth From Education

Save articles you read in blogs and instructional youtube videos to track how they influenced your Disc Golf Growth

Well-being

Mental & Physical Well-being Survey

Take a twice daily survey to record your mental and physical throughout your Disc Golf Growth. This can be used to find correlations in sleeping habits, happiness, and performance - all of which are closely tied together

Track Game Scores

See Cross Course Performance

Disc Golf Growth does more than collect your score each game. You can see how your score improves between and across courses as well as note what may be influencing your score such as particular training routines or getting enough sleep the night before

Math, Math, & More Math

Calculate Strokes Gained Statistics

Ever sinceUDisc added Strokes Gained the sport of Disc Golf has looked a lot more Money-Ball and it is important. Don’t be left behind with your abacus, join us in the future of the sport with statistics

Levels of Depth

Add A Little, Add A Lot

Whether simply want to write down your course and hole scores or you want to add the number of drives you took to get to circle 2, circle 1, then the basket. We have got tools for you to quickly get that in the app so you can get back to growing your game
